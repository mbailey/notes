# work from outdoors


Ideas for Working From Outdoors.

## General Considerations

- Laptop
    - bright screen?
    - large enough display
    - second screen?
- glare / reflection
    - choose a dark/shaded background to reduce contrast around screen
    - avoid light source (e.g. sun or bright reflective surface) behind
